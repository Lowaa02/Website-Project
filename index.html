Trivia

Amesame!!!

Gura is close friends with Watson Amelia. In one stream, Gura sang You Are My Sunshine but changed the lyrics to be about Amelia. The pair often collab together and are most commonly shipped together under the name and Twitter tag "#amesame".

Gawr Gura's intro theme was originally commissioned for Watson Amelia. Ame ended up not using the music, so Gura used it instead.

Her Genmate (Myth)

In Japanese, Gura's name is written 「がうる・ぐら」 ("gauru gura").

She gives her surname first, Japanese style, even when speaking in English. Takanashi Kiara and Ninomae Ina'nis also do this, while Calliope Mori and Amelia Watson use the English name order when speaking in English.

Her surname Gawr comes from the gar, an ancient holosteian order of ray-finned fish. Her first name Gura comes from the Latin gula, meaning "gluttony."

Gura is the only member of holoMyth to write her name entirely in hiragana. This is consistent with her childlike demeanor and academic skills, since hiragana-only writing is usually reserved for children's books.

Retirement...

Gawr Gura is known as the shark-girl VTuber to her many fans and nearly 4.6 million subscribers.In addition to gaming, Gawr Gura is known for her humor and the down to earth way she interacts with her fans. But everything changed on April 16, 2025. An emotional sounding Gura thanked her fans, expressing the joy she had while going from "being too shy to sing in the shower" to singing in front of "hundreds to thousands" of fans. She said her departure was due to issues with "management."

About her Mascot

Gura's mascot is named Bloop, a "long time friend who stays in a bubble 'for the better'." Bloop wears a can on his head, marked "emergency ration" 「非常食」. According to Gura in a multiple-choice question, Bloop is emergency food. In other instances, she has implied that Bloop isn't exactly a morally upstanding individual, and is likely bordering on evil based on how she described his plush version.

About her Fans

Her fanbase are officially called "Chumbuds.

Her channel members are referred to as MemberShrimp. This is a reference to Gura seeing her chat use the word "simp," to which she replied "I just saw the word 'simp' go by. What is simp? Do you mean shrimp?"

Her likes and dislikes

She likes food. She also enjoys a hobby known in Atlantis as "PWWIE", but she claims humans don't taste good so viewers don't have to worry.

Gura dislikes hot sand, and her biggest fear is having people hear her stomach noises, (which she wrote incorrectly in her debut stream as "loud stomak").
